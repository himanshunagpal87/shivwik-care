Who We Are

The Holistic Vision

Formerly known as "Holistic Care Centre", Shivwik Holistic Care was born out of a vision to bridge the gap between symptomatic treatment and long-term wellness.

We believe that recovery is more than just physical movement—it's about restoring the natural harmony of the body. Our facility in Paschim Vihar serves as a sanctuary where modern clinical expertise meets ancient healing traditions.

"Our mission is to empower patients with tools for self-healing, ensuring they return to their daily lives stronger and more resilient."

Dr. Sonal Khera Nagpal

MPT (Cardiopulmonary), BPT, NDDY

With an advanced Master’s in Physiotherapy specializing in Cardiopulmonary rehabilitation, Dr. Sonal brings clinical precision to every case. Her unique qualification in Naturopathy and Yoga (NDDY) allows her to design integrated recovery protocols that are rare in conventional clinics.
